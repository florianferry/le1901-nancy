Introduction to Art Nouveau

Art Nouveau, a major artistic movement of the late 19th century, reaches its peak in Nancy with the École de Nancy. Inspired by natural forms and curved lines, it transforms architecture and decorative arts.

Our apartment 'Le 1901' captures the essence of this style, offering a luxurious living space where elegance meets innovation. Discover how this movement influenced the city of Nancy and continues to inspire today.

History of the École de Nancy

Founded in 1901, the École de Nancy brings together artists and craftsmen who revolutionize decorative arts. Figures like Émile Gallé, Louis Majorelle, and the Daum brothers create works of incomparable beauty.

The movement promotes the integration of arts, where every everyday object becomes a work of art. From glassware to furniture to ironwork, Art Nouveau transforms Nancy's urban environment, leaving a lasting legacy.
